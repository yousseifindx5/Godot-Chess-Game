# Chess

A chess game made with Godot. You can play against a friend or against the computer.

## How to play

Run the game, pick a mode from the menu, and play. If you choose to play against the computer, you can pick Easy, Medium, or Hard before the game starts.

## Modes

**2 Players** — two people on the same machine, taking turns.

**vs Computer** — you play as white, the computer plays as black.

- Easy picks moves randomly
- Medium looks two moves ahead
- Hard looks three moves ahead

## Rules covered

The game handles all standard chess rules including castling, en passant, pawn promotion, check, checkmate, stalemate, the fifty-move rule, insufficient material, and threefold repetition.

## Options

You can adjust the music and sound effects volume from the options menu. It's accessible from the main menu and from inside the game.
